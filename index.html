SEGWAYFUN – SEGWAY AND ELECTRIC SCOOTER RIDES IN PRAGUE

MEET US AT THE CHARLES BRIDGE UNDER THE PRAGUE CASTLE

We are located in Mala strana, precisely in Mostecka 4. Charles bridge is just few steps from us, Lennon wall is behind the corner and Old town square is 15 minutes walk from us.
  • We organise e-scooter, Segway and e-bike tours in Prague daily in 7 languages.
  • We provide 3 hour tours from 1.500,- CZK and 90 minutes tours from 1,000,- CZK.
  • We are located next to the Charles Bridge on the Prague Castle side of the river.
SEGWAY TOURS

Prague on segway is definitely on the list of TOP 10 things to do in Prague. We offer 90-minute and 180-minute guided rides. In the parks surrounding Prague centre. During Segway Tours, you will visit, for example, Petřín Lookout Tower, Strahov Monastery or Břevnov Monastery – two places with the longest history of beer brewing in Bohemia – and many other places. The age limit is 8 years. Don’t you know what to do with children in Prague? Segway tour is an ideal activity for a family vacation.

ELECTRIC SCOOTER RIDES

Electric scooters represent a relatively new activity in Prague, which we initiated after the ban on segway in Prague centre. Their success is proven by positive reactions at Prague Tripadvisor. During the tour, you will visit major Prague attractions, such as Prague Castle, Old Town Square or Jewish quarter – Josefov.

RENTING ELECTRIC BICYCLES AND ELECTRIC SCOOTERS

During your vacation in Prague, you don’t have to follow guided city tours. Be your own boss and rent a bicycle or a scooter. We will provide you with a map of Prague, recommend nice places to visit, good restaurants with unique Prague atmosphere, and the rest is up to you. You can rent the bike for an hour or for the whole day.
